How to Bet on Hockey

A quick beginner’s guide to placing your first hockey bets.

4 Steps

  1. Choose a licensed sportsbook (see Where to Bet)
  2. Create your account and verify identity
  3. Deposit using a secure payment method
  4. Place your bet on a simple market like moneyline

Common Bet Types

  • Moneyline: Pick the winner. Example: Leafs -150 vs Canadiens +130
  • Puck Line: Spread, usually 1.5 goals. Example: Leafs -1.5 (+110)
  • Totals (Over/Under): Total goals. Example: Over 6.5 (-110)
  • Player Props: Individual stats (e.g., shots, points)
  • Parlays: Combine multiple bets for higher payout

Essential Tips

  • Use bankroll units (1–5% per bet)
  • Research goalies, injuries, and lineups
  • Don’t chase losses; be selective
  • Shop for the best odds across books

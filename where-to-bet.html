Where to Bet on Hockey

Choose licensed, reputable sportsbooks and understand your province’s rules.

Canada Overview

  • Ontario: Fully legal iGaming market (iGO). Multiple private operators.
  • BC: Government site (PlayNow).
  • Quebec: Government site (Mise‑o‑jeu).
  • Other Provinces: Provincial lotteries; private options vary.

What to Look For

  • Valid license and regulator info
  • Secure and fast payments
  • Good mobile app + live betting features
  • Responsive customer support

Red Flags

  • No license or unclear terms
  • Slow/no withdrawals
  • Poor reviews; no support
  • Unrealistic bonus promises

Complete Hockey Betting Guide

Strategies, seasonal factors, advanced stats, and common mistakes.

Key Strategies

  • Home Ice Advantage: Consider travel and time zones
  • Back‑to‑Backs: Fatigue affects second nights; watch totals
  • Goalies: Check confirmed starters and recent form
  • Line Combinations: Injuries and PP units matter

Seasonal Factors

  • Early season: chemistry forming; often higher scoring
  • Mid season: stable trends; watch trade deadline
  • Late season: motivation and rest patterns shift
  • Playoffs: tighter games; goaltending critical

Advanced Stats

  • Corsi% / Fenwick% — shot attempt share
  • PDO — shooting% + save% (~100 is average)
  • xG — expected goals, chance quality

Common Mistakes

  • Betting fav teams emotionally
  • Ignoring goalie matchups
  • Chasing losses
  • Betting every game

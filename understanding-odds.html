Understanding Hockey Betting Odds

Three common formats: American, Decimal, Fractional.

American Odds

  • Favorite: -150 means bet $150 to win $100
  • Underdog: +130 means bet $100 to win $130

Decimal Odds

  • Shows total return: 1.67 means $100 returns $167 (profit $67)

Fractional Odds

  • Profit to stake ratio: 5/6 means win $5 for every $6 bet

Example Payout (Leafs -120)

  • American: Profit = 100 ÷ 120 × $100 ≈ $83.33; Return ≈ $183.33
  • Decimal 1.83: Return = 1.83 × $100 = $183
  • Fractional 5/6: Profit = (5 ÷ 6) × $100 ≈ $83.33; Return ≈ $183.33

Key Concepts

  • Implied Probability: Lower odds = higher implied probability
  • Vig/Juice: The bookmaker’s commission
  • Line Shopping: Compare multiple sportsbooks
